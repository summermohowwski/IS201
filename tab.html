SCUBA DIVING

For the past 12 years I have been an avid scuba diver. I have traveled to many different countries and swam with amazing creatures. Here are the top 3 reasons why I love diving!


  1. It is breathtaking
    • The fish are cool
    • The water is cool
    • The coral is cool
  2. Every place you dive is unique
    • Every dive site is different
    • Fish act differently
    • You meet different people
  3. IT IS FUN
    • You learn something new
    • Great times with friends and family
    • Create lasting memories
